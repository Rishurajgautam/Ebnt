overview

The utilization of renewable energy sources is having a significant influence on the development of rural communities and the lives of farmers. As a result of breakthroughs in energy technology made possible by nature, landowners and farmers worldwide now have access to profitable new opportunities. As a private renewable energy operator, EBNT has a unique depth of knowledge and a customer-centric approach to solar and wind energy production systems.

Innovating a greener future

Solar and wind farms developed by EBNT will provide enormous benefits for a variety of stakeholders, including farmers, rural communities, landowners, and others.

  • New Sources of Income
  • Employment Opportunities and Training in the Construction, Technical, Customer Service, and Maintenance Sectors
  • New Tax revenue for Community Development
  • Community Development and support

Many people still need to understand how solar and wind power function, even though they're becoming more crucial for the economy and the environment. Our mission at EBNT with Energy as a Service (EaaS) is to provide a tailor-made, ready-to-implement solution for your property so that you and your neighbors may live in better harmony with the natural world.

EBNT plans, builds, finances, and manages your project as part of its Energy as a Service (EaaS) offering. This is done with sensitivity for the local community, farmers, landowners, and the environment.

Identify The Potential

By employing environmental professionals with knowledge of standard operating procedures, we aim to reduce the adverse effects of our activities on natural areas and the wildlife that inhabit there.

Once a site fits the requirements for a solar or wind array, we plan meetings with potential landowners or farmers to discuss the project, give them maps of the area, explain the science behind solar and wind energy, and outline the financial benefits to them.

Getting To Know Each Other
Our relationships with the farmers and landowners whose property we use are our priority. Farmers and landowners will have access to a crew of construction specialists when the time comes to construct. We make use of every opportunity, from casual encounters to formal get-togethers to written correspondence, to keep the lines of communication open.
Project Review

Prior to construction, we consult with landowners or farmers to plan out the access routes and the installation of solar farms or wind turbines. Keeping with state, regional, and national regulations, the solar or wind farm is planned to provide a safe and appropriate buffer zone between the array and nearby homes, businesses, and roads.

Construction Overview

Construction comprises of building access roads and clearing the ground for the solar farm or establishing the foundations for the wind turbine towers. The following

  • 1. Solar farms need rack systems to hold the solar panels, power inverters, batteries, charge controllers, and cabling for wiring the system, to mention a few of the system's essential components.
  • 2. The installation of the wind turbine tower modules, nacelles, blades, and the construction of the storage and water capture tanks, differs depending on the wind turbine utilized.
