Commercial / Industries Buildings

Solar energy systems provide major financial advantages to the owners of commercial and industrial buildings. Solar energy has the potential to cut or even eliminate the cost of power, leading to an increase in cash flow and more predictable monthly energy expenses.

Commercial Buildings

Solar energy offers commercial building owners a variety of return-on-investment alternatives. Solar energy is an effective way to earn LEED points for both new construction and the retrofitting of existing buildings, and it also helps reduce future energy costs. Compared to your current electric utility supplier, this results in the highest potential output and the biggest possible monthly savings for a commercial property owner. EBNT is equipped to make your business stand out from the competition in almost any commercial construction project. Integrating a high-performance solar energy system into the building might improve the company's public relations.

When it comes to the enormous field of commercial real estate, no two properties are ever identical in any way. There is a strong correlation between the kind of business that occupies a commercial space and the level of demand for that space. Our highly trained design and technical team can tailor a solution to practically any problem in the most effective way possible. We will handle everything else; all we want from you is your input on any unforeseen limitations on space.

Solar Canopies

Solar canopies provide a simple and more affordable alternative to roof-mounted solar energy systems that are complex and expensive. They make it possible for companies to optimize the usage of large parking lots for electricity production while simultaneously satisfying the shade demands of employees, customers, and other guests who frequent the establishment.

Solar Roof-Mounted
The roof-mounted solar solutions offered by EBNT have been devised to realize the greatest possible financial savings. This is accomplished by transforming the ample roof space available into a channel for producing clean energy. We can develop systems that need very little upkeep and will endure for a long time.
