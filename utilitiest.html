UTILITIES / POWER CONSUMPTION

The team at EBNT is at the frontline of solar energy's evolution at every stage of the process: research and development, building, and running. With a dedication to environmental sustainability, EBNT works with public and commercial utilities to create new and improved solar designs.

We construct and operate sustainable solar systems and can do the same for you. In addition to developing our projects, we help our partners with land and entitlement acquisition, power purchase agreement (PPA) and finance negotiations, engineering, interconnection processing, procurement, and design construction services.

Partners provide round-the-clock monitoring, emphasizing real-time tracking, and a suite of maintenance services and tools, such as solar panel cleaning programs. EBNT has developed a funding initiative to finance energy projects to better help our customers with the many aspects of solar and wind renewable energy systems. We conduct on-site training sessions with "run books" to facilitate a smooth handoff and transfer of responsibility to operation and maintenance teams.
