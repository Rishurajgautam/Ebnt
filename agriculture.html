AGRICULTURE

When it comes to solar power for the agricultural sector, look no further than EBNT. Because the sun provides 99.9 percent of the world's energy, it's clear that solar power is an essential renewable resource for the agriculture industry. For these and other applications where sunshine is critical, ENBT provides reliable and skilled solar professionals. Our staff is well-versed in agribusinesses' seasonal and widely varying energy needs. To provide the most suitable solar equipment and installation, we examine your electricity consumption, facility use patterns, and other relevant variables.

Cultivating the Future

Agricultural businesses have shown a substantial pace of interest in adopting solar energy as a source of electricity. Recent technological advancements have spurred this enthusiasm. Solar energy is an attractive investment option, which should not come as a surprise given that it is significantly more cost-effective than fossil fuels. In addition, solar energy has the additional benefit of having predictable energy bills for decades to come, which is a significant advantage. Because of the abundance of sunlight, state programs, and federal incentives, the possibility of lowering or getting rid of one's monthly energy bills has immediate benefits for one's bottom line and has a very quick payback time.

After installing solar panels, you will no longer be at the discretion of your utility company in the event of a power outage due to drought or other natural disasters. In addition, the local and regional economies may benefit from agricultural activities that shift to sustainable energy.

Efficient Metering

Solar has very seldom been a viable option for users with several pumps and meters. Due to recent legislation in the United States and other countries, farmers and producers may now adopt net energy metering. Using net energy metering aggregation, owners of solar systems or other renewable-producing facilities may seek energy generation credit from a single solar installation across many metered accounts. Previously, it was not possible for a single solar energy system to balance the electrical energy consumption of buildings that are not directly connected to the solar power system. Energy BY Nature Technologies (EBNT) uses these rules by designing and installing a single, efficient, turnkey solar energy system for several properties. This solar system's efficient design will significantly lower your daily operating expenditures.

Operations and Maintenance
We recognize that you cannot afford downtime and have no desire to become a solar maintenance business. EBNT will be at your side throughout the process, from planning and design through installation and monitoring to a comprehensive study of your energy use and power costs. A company's energy requirements will be satisfied, and its owners will get attentive, quick service centered on their demands.
