overview

Solar energy, one of the world's most efficient and commonly used renewable energy sources, is an excellent option for farms, companies, and governments looking to decrease energy costs and move toward sustainability. Using solar power in conjunction with batteries and energy storage to build a more dependable, secure, and sustainable energy system might provide your company with a competitive advantage. EBNT is always looking for new solar and storage solutions to help our clients and the grid operators to whom they provide electricity.

Our team and partners have created, and managed solar energy projects for schools, municipalities,and private organizations as an integrator of clean technologies focused on energy efficiency and renewable energy. When you work with EBNT, you have access to a team of solar power experts that will help you choose the best path to success.

We provide comprehensive services, starting with system design and concluding with a real-time digital dashboard for production monitoring and ongoing support. EBNT is capable of managing all facets of a solar power plant, including design, building, ownership, operation, financing, and maintenance. Our solar personnel and partners are well-versed in the regulations and can help establish municipal, utility, state, and federal laws. On behalf of our clients, we obtained the required permits and subsidies to keep projects on schedule and under budget.

Solar For Any Location

Solar Farm

Open space, brownfields, and landfills are all potentially suitable for solar development, but it all depends on the specifics of your site. Our company and its partners have years of experience developing environmentally sensitive locations into brownfield solar farms.

Often referred to as "shared solar," the term "community solar" describes a solar farm that serves several clients. Community solar projects provide net metering credits that locals and companies may utilize to offset their energy use and lower their carbon footprint the use of Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs).

Canopies
Solar canopies from EBNT may be constructed over parking lots, fields, pathways, and even specific plants to produce power without interfering with the environment below. Supporting solar energy may increase your company's visibility and save you money..
Roof Top
Solar panel arrays that are placed on rooftops may be installed on a wide range of various sorts of buildings. It may or may not be possible for you to generate all of the energy you need from the sun, depending on the size of your roof and the ways in which you utilize energy. Rooftops have the potential to become a source of cash in some locations owing to the possibility of selling surplus power back to the electrical grid in certain locations.
Off-Grid
Solar power systems that operate independently from the grid give an abundance of alternative energy sources in locations that are geographically remote and do not have the ability to support the construction of an electrical infrastructure due to exorbitant costs or infeasibility. Producing electricity in any area that has access to sunshine is now technically viable due to the increasing availability of off-grid solar systems that provide high levels of efficiency.
